Smart plant watering system

About our System

Our smart plant watering system is designed to make it easy for you to care for your plants, even if you have a busy schedule.
With its innovative technology, the system provides the perfect amount of water for your plants to thrive, without overwatering or underwatering them.
You can customize the settings to suit the specific needs of your plants, and receive alerts on your phone if anything needs your attention.
Our system also tracks environmental factors like temperature and humidity, and checks the soil moisture.
With our smart plant watering system, you can enjoy healthy, vibrant plants all year round, without any of the guesswork.

Humidity

Humidity sensors can play an important role in a smart outdoor watering system by helping to ensure that plants receive the appropriate amount of water. These sensors measure the amount of moisture in the air and soil, and can communicate this information to the watering system.

Soil Moisture

Soil moisture is a critical factor in plant health, and a smart outdoor watering system can help ensure that plants receive the right amount of water by monitoring soil moisture levels.

Temperature

We measure temperature because we know how temperature affects plant health and growth. Extreme temperature causes plants to experience dehydration; by monitoring the temperature, the sensor helps assess whether the plants are exposed to unfavorable conditions, enabling appropriate adjustments to the watering schedule.

Water Level Sensors

Our water level sensor is a device that is used to measure and monitor the level of water in a tank, reservoir, or any other water storage system. It provides real-time information about the water level, allowing for efficient management and control of water resources.
